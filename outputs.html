Outputs

What we are making together — before, during, and after the residency.

The Book

Following the residency, we are gathering submissions for a post-residency chapbook. The book will document the work, ideas, and questions that emerged from our ten days together at Stroanfreggan House. It is open to all participants and collaborators.

Call for Submissions

We welcome contributions in any form that engages with the themes of the residency: emergence, complexity, the relationship between parts and wholes, generativity, and the conditions under which something new comes into being.

Accepted formats

  • Short essay or critical writing (up to 3,000 words)
  • Fiction, poetry, or other literary work
  • Artwork, photographs, or visual documentation
  • Musical score, audio notation, or written description of a musical work
  • Technical or scientific writing
  • Hybrid, experimental, or other formats — please get in touch first

Guidelines

  • Submissions may be new work made during or after the residency, or existing work you feel speaks to the themes.
  • All contributors will retain rights to their own work.
  • Contributions will be lightly edited for consistency. Significant editorial changes will be made in conversation with you.
  • Images should be submitted at 300 dpi or higher where possible.

Deadline: April 1, 2026

Submit via email to the organisers, or drop files into the shared folder distributed during the residency.

The Magazine

Beginning in March 2026, we are meeting weekly online for three months to curate a quarterly magazine documenting ongoing work and outcomes from the residency. The magazine will be published digitally and distributed to all participants.

If you are a participant and want to contribute to the editorial process, please let the organisers know.
