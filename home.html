emergence

Winter Quarter 2026 • mid-February (10 days) • Stroanfreggan House, Dumfriesshire, Scotland

An interdisciplinary, mostly-offline residency to explore how simple rules create complex worlds—through reading, making, silence, and collaboration.

About

We’ll bring together artists, engineers, writers, scientists, philosophers, and cross-disciplinary makers to ask when a whole becomes more than the sum of its parts—and what that emergence looks like.

Outcomes

  • Co-create a new work: art, performance, writing, music, or inquiry.
  • Contribute to a chapbook (due April 1, 2026) and prepare a 1-page bio of prior work.
  • Meet weekly for three months post-residency to curate a quarterly magazine documenting outcomes.

Preparatory reading (bring one source for Reading Day)

  • Philip Anderson — “More Is Different” (1972)
  • Melanie Mitchell — Complexity: A Guided Tour (or Krakauer’s Intro to Complexity Papers)
  • Italo Calvino — Cosmicomics; Olga Tokarczuk — Flights; Ursula K. Le Guin — The Dispossessed / Always Coming Home
  • Agnes Denes — Wheatfield — A Confrontation; James Turrell — Skyspaces; Casey Reas / Manfred Mohr; Cunningham–Cage collaborations

Micro-experiments

Each participant brings a small seed (algorithm, improvisation score, poem fragment, visual prompt) for others to iterate on—creating emergent works in real time.

Location & logistics

Stroanfreggan House (converted 1930s school; Brander family since the 1970s) has a great room with wood-burning stove, dining room & stocked kitchen. Dorm-style rooms (2–4 per room), two bathrooms with showers. The area offers an Iron Age hill fort, woodsy walks, and Dark Sky stargazing.

Weather: Cold, windy, and dark; rarely below freezing. Dress warmly. Underfloor heating; great room heated by wood stove.

Travel

  • Airports: Edinburgh (EDI), Glasgow (GLA). Pickup from Dumfries train station or shuttle from Edinburgh depending on arrivals.
  • Car required. One rental car available for local trips (licensed drivers/registered visitors only).
  • Grocery delivery on day 1; further runs as needed. Travel & fuel billed based on use.

Connectivity

Limited-connectivity residency. No consistent internet. Staff keep hotspot phones for emergencies only. Resident phones collected on arrival and returned 24h before departure.

Budget (estimate)

  • Stroanfreggan rental: £2,000
  • Rental car: £50/day → ~£650 incl. fuel
  • Food: £15/person/day → ~£150/person
  • 15% contingency → £5,115 total ≈ £511/person for 10 days (travel to/from residency not included)
