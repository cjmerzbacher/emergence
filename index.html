About

We’re gathering artists, engineers, writers, scientists, philosophers, and cross-disciplinary thinkers to ask: when is a whole more than the sum of its parts? What does it look like when properties emerge?

Outcomes

  • Co-create a new work: art, performance, writing, music, or inquiry.
  • Contribute a short piece to a chapbook (due April 1, 2026).
  • After the residency, meet weekly for three months to curate a quarterly magazine documenting the work.
  • Each resident prepares a 1-page bio of prior work (text and/or visuals).

Preparatory Reading (bring one source for Reading Day)

  • Philip Anderson — “More Is Different” (1972)
  • Melanie Mitchell — Complexity: A Guided Tour (or Krakauer’s Intro to Complexity Papers)
  • Italo Calvino — Cosmicomics; Olga Tokarczuk — Flights; Ursula K. Le Guin — The Dispossessed or Always Coming Home
  • Agnes Denes — Wheatfield — A Confrontation; James Turrell — Skyspaces; generative artists Casey Reas / Manfred Mohr; Cunningham/Cage collaborations

Micro-experiments

Each participant brings a small seed (algorithm, score, poem fragment, visual prompt) that others can iterate on. We’ll make emergent works in real time.

Location & Logistics

Stroanfreggan House (converted 1930s school; Brander family since the 1970s) has a great room with wood-burning stove, a dining room and stocked kitchen, dorm-style housing (2–4 per room), and two bathrooms with showers. The surrounding area offers an Iron Age hill fort and woodsy walks; it’s in a Dark Sky Area—excellent winter stargazing.

Weather: Cold, windy, and dark; rarely below freezing. Dress warmly. Underfloor heating throughout; great room is heated by wood stove.

Travel

  • Nearest airports: Edinburgh (EDI), Glasgow (GLA). Pickup from Dumfries train station or shuttle from Edinburgh depending on arrivals.
  • Car required for access; a rental car will be available for local trips (licensed drivers/registered visitors only).
  • Grocery delivery arranged day 1; additional runs as needed. Travel & fuel billed based on use.

Connectivity

Limited-connectivity residency. No consistent internet at Stroanfreggan. Staff will keep hotspot phones for emergencies only. Resident phones collected on day 1 and returned 24h before departure.

Budget (estimate)

  • Stroanfreggan rental: £2,000
  • Rental car: £50/day → ~£650 incl. fuel
  • Food: £15/person/day → ~£150/person
  • 15% contingency → £5,115 total ≈ £511/person for 10 days (travel to/from residency not included)

FAQ

Accessibility

The house is multi-room with shared facilities; please let us know about specific needs and we’ll coordinate.

Can I be online?

We’re intentionally offline to support focus. Emergency connectivity is available via staff phones.

What should I bring?

Warm layers, sturdy shoes, personal meds, and a small “micro-experiment” for others to build on.
